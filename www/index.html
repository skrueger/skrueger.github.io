About me

I'm a software engineer.

I work for Microsoft on the contact service for Skype, Windows Phone, and other Microsoft products.

Before that I worked on the Live Connect platform. I wrote client libraries for Android, Windows Phone, Windows 8, and JavaScript. This work can be found on GitHub.

In 2009, I was an intern at Mozilla where I worked inside on their metric team. I analyzing various aspects and products of Mozilla. My final presentation

In 2008, I interned at Yahoo! in University of Illionis's Research Park. I worked on two projects. An internal web application to visualize their datacenters and a Hadoop MapReduce job to replace a stage in their advertisement data pipeline processing system.
